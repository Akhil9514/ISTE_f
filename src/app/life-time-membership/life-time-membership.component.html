ELIGIBILITY FOR MEMBERSHIP

Bye-Laws 2 (ii) and (iii) of the Society read as under:

(ii) Life Membership

  1. A person who satisfies the eligibility critera as mentioned in clause 2 (iii) may become a life member of ISTE by paying the admissible Life Membership fee as decided from time to time, by the Executive Council.

(iii) Membership : Eligibility Criteria:

  1. Any person of Indian Origin or a foreign National holding a University Degree/Diploma or an equivalent qualification and who is/had been employed on the teaching faculty of a recognised technical institution; or a person having a technical academic qualification working in a Technical/Research Institute is ELIGIBLE for Membership
  2. Application for membership shall be made on the prescribed form of the Society.
  3. Application for Membership shall be recommended by a Life Member of the Society.
  4. Such application shall be approved the Executive Council or by an Office Bearer of the Society authorized by the Executive Council for this purpose before the membership is approved.
  5. The Executive Council shall bave the power to refuse admission to any applicant without assigning any reason.
