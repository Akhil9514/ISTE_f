Guidelines

There is a limited budgetary provision for the STTPs under the overall QIP Scheme of the MHRD/ AICTE. Therefore, in order to enable the teachers to enhance their technical competencies and help them to become eligible for Career Advancement opportunities, STTPs on self-financing basis are introduced. The special features of the Self Financing STTPs are as under:

  • These are programmes being conducted locally.
  • The participating teacher is not required to take leave.
  • The academic schedule of the institution is not affected.
  • Boarding & lodging of participants are not required.
  • TA/DA expenses are zero.
  • The expenses to be incurred on Guest Experts, Instructional Material, etc. could be met by the concerned institute itself.
  • The nominal course fee of Rs. 1180/- (1000 + 18% GST) per participants to be necessarily paid to the ISTE Headquarters after completion of the programme.
