ISTE is a National, professional organization with membership open to all sectors including corporate sectors connected with or interested in Industry-Academia collaboration. Its major objective is to assist and contribute to the production and development of professional / Managers/Engineers and technicians of the best quality as needed by the Industry and other organizations.

In planning its role effectively, ISTE has to develop good linkages and bridge industry –academia gap and with this mind, ISTE has introduces “ Corporate Institutional Membership” for Corporate Sectors, Pubic or Pvt. Industry, PSUs including Maharatna/Navratna/Miniratna PSUs involved in IT/Consulting/Construction/Mfg./Oil & gas/petroleum/Energy/Power/ Pharma/ Automotives/ Automobile/FMCG/software etc

Corporate Institutional Membership is for Corporate Sector/Industrial Organization for effective Industry-Academia Partnership. The fee is Rs, 1,18,000/- (incl GST).

WE THEREFORE, REQUEST ALL CORPORATE SECTORS/PSUs, INDUSTRY AND OTHER ORGANISATION TO BECOME CORPORATE INSTITUTIONAL MEMBER OF THE ISTE AND THEREBY HELP TO IMPROVE THE QUALITY AND RELEVANCE OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION.

CORPORATE INSTITUTIONAL MEMBERSHIP IS VALID FOR LIFE TIME.
