Students of Civil/Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics/ Chemical/ Agricultural branch who are doing M.Tech. degree thesis work can apply for the award and send ONE COPY of the thesis with a two page abstract of the original contributions made in the thesis to ISTE. These awards will be distributed during the ISTE Students National Convention every year.
