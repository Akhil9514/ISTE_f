Details of ISTE Student Conventions

  • First Annual Convention of ISTE Students, BIET, Davengare (Karnataka), 11 & 12 October, 1998 – Role of Technical Students in Nation Building. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Dr. S. Rame Gowda, Chairman, AICTE, New Delhi.
  • Second Annual Convention of ISTE Students, SES College of Engineering, Kopargaon (Maharashtra), 30 & 31, October, 1999 – Challenges for Technical Students in the 21st Century. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Dr. Vinod N. Walivadekar, Director, CEDT, Aurangabad.
  • Third Annual Convention of ISTE Students, Regional Engineering College, Jalandhar (Punjab), 14 & 15, October, 2000 – The Role of Information Technology in Technical Education. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Sardar Jagdish Singh Garcha, Minister for Technical Education and Industrial Training, Govt. of Punjab.
  • Fourth Annual Convention of ISTE Students, Kongu Engineering College, Perundurai, Erode (T.N.), 28 & 29 July, 2001 – Harnessing Human Resources for the Development of India: Problems, Challenges and Opportunities. Chief Guest: Prof. N.R. Shelly, Member Secretary, IGNCA, New Delhi.
  • Fifth Annual Convention of ISTE Students, Sant Longowal Institute of Engg. & Technology, Longowal, Sangrur (Punjab), 3 & 4 October, 2002 – Social Responsibility of Technical Institutions in Rural Development. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Shri Sukhdev Singh Dhindsa, Minister for Chemicals and Fertilisers, Govt. of India.
  • Sixth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – JNTU, Hyderabad, 10-11 October, 2003 – Tuning Students to New Technology Developments. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Shri Nerella Anjaneyalu, Minister of Technical Education, Govt. of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Seventh Annual Convention of ISTE Students – B & B Institute of Technology, Vallabh Vidyanagar (Gujarat), 30-31 October, 2004 – Vision 2020: Role of Engineering & Technology.
  • Eighth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Baba Banda Singh Bahadur Engineering College, Fatehgarh Sahib, Punjab, 21-23 October, 2005 – Impact of Technology on Societal Living. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Sardar Parkash Singh Badal, President, Shiromani Akali Dal and Former Chief Minister, Punjab.
  • Ninth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Adichunchanagiri Institute of Technology, Chikmagalur, Karnataka, October 6-7, 2006 – The Role of Technical Education in Societal Transformation. Chief Guest: Dr. N. Rudraiah, former Vice Chancellor, Gulbarga University.
  • Tenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – G.M.R. Institute of Technology, Rajam, Andhra Pradesh, October 5-6, 2007 – Technical Education Scenario in the Changing Paradigm of Globalisation. Chief Guest: Dr. L. Venugopala Reddy, Vice Chancellor, Andhra University, Visakhapatnam.
  • Eleventh Annual Convention of ISTE Students – National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, October 11-12, 2008 – Skill Sets Development and Employability of Students in Technical Education. Chief Guest: Prof R.S. Nirjar, former President, ISTE and Vice Chancellor, Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida.
  • Twelfth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, Sathyamangalam, Erode Dist., Tamil Nadu, October 4-5, 2009 – Global Opportunities and Challenges of Technical Students. Chief Guest: Prof. M. Chidambaram, Director, National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirapalli.
  • Thirteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Jaipur Engineering College, Kukas, Jaipur (Rajasthan), October 2-3, 2010 – Nurturing Creativity among Potential Professionals through Technical Education. Chief Guest: Prof. N.V. Vasani, Vice Chancellor, Nirma University, Ahmedabad.
  • Fourteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Jayam College of Engineering & Technology, Dharmapuri (TN), September 30th and October 1, 2011 – Green Engineering for Sustainable Development. Chief Guest: Dr. M.S. Palanichamy, Vice Chairman, Tamilnadu State Council for Technical Education.
  • Fifteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Sou. Sushila Danchand Ghodawat Charitable Trust’s Sanjay Ghodawat Group of Institutions, Atigre, Dist. Kolhapur (Maharashtra), October 12th & 13th, 2012 – Technocrats Vision in Sculptin India Super Power. Chief Guest: Dr. N.R. Shetty, Former President, ISTE.
  • Sixteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Periyar Manniammai University, Thanjavur, Vallam (Tamil Nadu), October 27th & 28th, 2013 – Social Responsibility of Technical Students in Environmental Sustainability. Chief Guest: Dr. K. Veeramani, Chancellor, Periyar Maniammai University, Thanjavur.
  • Seventeenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – V.V.P. Engineering College, Rajkot (Gujarat), October 10th & 11th, 2014 – Sustainable Developments through Innovative Technologies. Chief Guest: Hon’ble Shri Om Prakash Kohli, Governor of Gujarat.
  • Eighteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Kings College of Engineering, Punalkulam, Thanjavur Dist. (Tamil Nadu), October 30th & 31st, 2015 – Technology for Rural Development to Accomplish Vision India – 2020.
  • Nineteenth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – VIT University, Vellore, October 15-16, 2016, Evolving Technical Trends in India Meet Challenges Beyond 2025.
  • Twentieth Annual Convention of ISTE Students – Regional Institute of Science & Technology, Meghalaya (Assam), October 28th & 29th, 2017
  • Twentyfirst Annual Convention of ISTE Students – L.D. College of Engineering, Ahmedabad, Ahmedabad (Gujarat), January 29th – 30th, 2019 – Empowering India Through Innovations.
