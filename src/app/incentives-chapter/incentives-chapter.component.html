T.A. for attending ISTE Convention

Chapter which complete at least 6 Technical Activities (3 of them with outside experts/faculty) for the members during January to October will be given to and fro Second Class Railway fare to attend the Annual Convention of ISTE to be held during November/December. The following norms will be followed for the incentive, in addition to the requirement of organizing the above-mentioned 6 technical activities:

CHAPTERS Fare Details
Polytechnic Chapters with at Least 25 Life Members One Second Class to and from rail fare
Polytechnic Chapters with more than 50 Life Members Two Second Class to and from rail fare
Engineering College (and other degree level institution) Chapters with at least 50 Life Members One Second Class to and from rail fare
Engineering College Chapters with more than 100 Life Members Two Second Class to and from rail fare

Note: All Chapters should complete 6 Technical activities as mentioned above. For claiming the incentives, please use Form 6 (page 39). No other Form of claim will be entertained.

Special Awards to Chapters

Special Awards will be given during the ISTE annual Convention to the Best ISTE Chapter in the various ISTE Sections as judged by the respective Section Managing Committees. The following terms of reference may be followed in deciding the award:

  • Total number and variety of activities
  • The relevance of the activities to the members
  • General leadership exhibited
  • Resources generated locally
  • Extension services rendered

Note:

  1. There will be awards for Best Chapter and Best Students Chapter
  2. Sections are requested to finalise the Awards at the earliest and inform the name of the Best Chapter before September.
  3. Only Sections having 15 or more ISTE Chapters are eligible for the Best Chapter Award. Similarly only Sections with 10 or more Students Chapters are eligible for Best Students Chapter Award.

Note : Awards Cannot be shared between two or more Chapters.
