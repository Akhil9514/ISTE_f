The life Members will be issued plastic laminated photo cards. The Life Members who have been issued card-board cards earlier could also get the newly introduced plastic cards by way of making a payment of Rs. 100/- by way of a DD in favour of ISTE New Delhi payable at New Delhi. In addition, Life Members are given Membership Certificates. The lost or damaged Membership Plastic Cards & Certificates will be replaced only on payment of Rs. 50/- & Rs. 50/- respectively.
